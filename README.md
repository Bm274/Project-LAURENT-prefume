# Project LAURENT prefume
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/appsmithorg/appsmith/release/static/appsmith_logo_primary.png)

LAURENT Perfumes

LAURENT is an independent perfume house that creates unique fragrances designed to captivate the senses and reflect each individual’s personality in a refined and distinctive way. The company blends art and science in the craft of perfumery to develop original compositions that combine luxury, elegance, and exclusivity at competitive prices, making an unforgettable fragrance experience accessible to all.

Each fragrance by LAURENT tells its own story, creates lasting memories, and reflects the identity of its wearer—transforming perfume from a simple scent into a personal expression of style and sophistication.

Business Problem:
perfume companies face challenges in reaching a wider audience online, as many of them rely primarily on in-store sales.
Customers also struggle to discover unique fragrances, learn about their ingredients and prices, and compare different options without physically visiting the store.
many small businesses lack a strong digital platform to present their products in an attractive and engaging way, resulting in a limited and impersonal online shopping experience.
Customers also lack tools that help them choose perfumes that match their personality or preferences, which may reduce customer satisfaction and repeat purchases.

Proposed Solution:
This project aims to design a web application for LAURENT that provides customers with a complete and integrated online perfume shopping experience.
Through the platform, customers will be able to:
Explore the company’s exclusively designed perfumes through an attractive and elegant interface.
View detailed information about each fragrance, including ingredients, fragrance notes, and prices.
Browse perfumes by category (Men, Women, Unisex) or based on preferences.
Add products to a shopping cart and complete the purchase process easily and securely.
Discover the story behind each fragrance and the brand identity, making the shopping experience more personal and distinctive.

In this way, the website will not only function as an online store, but also as a comprehensive digital platform that reflects the luxurious identity of LAURENT and delivers a unique and memorable fragrance experience.
